How I Work

Structured, calm, and accountable in high-pressure environments.

1

Comfortable with complexity

Used to operating in environments where multiple priorities run in parallel — whether regulatory, operational, or transformation-focused — without losing control of governance or delivery rhythm.

2

Strong in recovery and stabilisation

A meaningful part of my value comes in situations where programmes need structure, traction, and sharper execution. I’m comfortable stepping into ambiguity and helping bring order to work that has started to drift.

3

End-to-end ownership mindset

I’m most effective when I can stay close to the full lifecycle of delivery — from planning and governance through execution, cutover, and post-go-live stabilisation — rather than treating delivery as complete at launch.

4

Cross-industry judgement

Working across four industries has helped me recognise delivery patterns early — especially around governance gaps, stakeholder friction, and execution risk — even when the business context is different.

5

Structured, but not process-heavy

I believe in discipline, clarity, and accountability — but not bureaucracy for its own sake. The goal is always better delivery, not more administration.

6

Curious about AI, grounded in delivery reality

I’m actively exploring where AI and LLM tools can genuinely improve programme and PMO work — especially in reporting, documentation, knowledge reuse, and workflow efficiency — without losing the judgement that experienced delivery still requires.
